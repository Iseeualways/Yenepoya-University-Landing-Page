About Us

Why Choose Yenepoya University

Yenepoya (Deemed-to-be University) was established with the primary objective of providing quality higher education by integrating the latest methods of pedagogy and modern principles of academia with the highest ethical standards. This approach aims to foster individuals who can help extend the acquired knowledge to develop various communities and society as a whole. Yenepoya University has earned several accolades — including rankings, recognitions, and awards — for its comprehensive approach to nurturing well-rounded students.

Yenepoya University, Bhartiya City

The Yenepoya University Bangalore Campus is a constituent unit of Yenepoya (Deemed-to-be University). Established in 2023, it is located in one of Bangalore’s most elite areas, Bhartiya City. The university offers courses in various fields of study, including computer science, applied science, commerce and management, allied health, and more, in collaboration with acclaimed industry partners and reputed institutes. It also utilises innovative learning strategies to promote experiential learning. Additionally, the campus is equipped with smart classrooms, laboratories, libraries, student learning zones, and more.

Admission Process

Your Journey Starts Here

A transparent, streamlined process designed to make your admission experience seamless.

01

Application Submission

Complete our comprehensive online application with personal details, academic qualifications, and program preferences. Upload all required documentation including mark sheets and certificates.

02

Entrance Examination

Appear for NEET or relevant entrance examinations based on your chosen program. We accept scores from national and state-level examinations.

03

Counseling Process

Attend counseling sessions either in person or online. Our expert admission team guides you through program selection and seat allocation based on your performance.

04

Document Verification

Submit original documents for verification including educational certificates, identity proof, and medical fitness certificate at our campus.

05

Enrollment & Fee Payment

Complete the fee payment process and finalize your enrollment. Receive your student ID and gain access to university facilities and resources.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria for MBBS admission? +
Candidates must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks. NEET qualification is mandatory with the required cutoff score as per government norms.
Does Yenepoya University provide hostel facilities? +
Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, mess facilities, common rooms, and 24/7 security. Accommodation is available on a first-come, first-served basis.
What is the fee structure for different programs? +
Fee structure varies by program. MBBS fees range from ₹18-20 lakhs per year, BDS from ₹10-12 lakhs per year, and allied health programs from ₹1-3 lakhs per year. Detailed fee structure is available in our prospectus.
Are scholarships available for students? +
Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students. Scholarships range from 10% to 50% tuition fee waiver based on academic performance and entrance exam scores.
What are the placement opportunities after graduation? +
We have a dedicated placement cell with partnerships at leading hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, and healthcare organizations. Our placement rate exceeds 95% with attractive packages. We also support higher education pursuits abroad.
