About Yenepoya University

Yenepoya (Deemed-to-be-University) is a leading institution in Karnataka, known for delivering quality higher education with a strong focus on innovation, research, and global competencies. Headquartered in Mangalore, the university offers a multidisciplinary learning environment set in a serene campus that promotes academic excellence and holistic development.

Expanding its legacy, the Yenepoya Bangalore campus brings the university’s academic excellence to a dynamic urban setting, offering industry-aligned programs across management, technology, commerce, and allied healthcare. Designed as a state-of-the-art learning hub, the campus features modern infrastructure, experiential learning centres, and collaborations with leading global organizations to prepare students for the future of work.

Across both campuses, Yenepoya is committed to nurturing skilled professionals and responsible leaders through a blend of academic rigor, practical exposure, and community engagement thereby empowering students to thrive in an increasingly global and technology-driven world.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Yenepoya University? +
Yenepoya (Deemed-to-be University) is an A+ NAAC-accredited university based in Karnataka with campuses in Mangalore and Bangalore. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across healthcare, medicine, technology, management, and allied sciences, with a strong focus on academic excellence, practical learning, and industry relevance.
What is the difference between the Mangalore and Bangalore campuses? +
The Mangalore campus primarily focuses on medical, dental, pharmacy, nursing, and allied health sciences, while the Bangalore campus offers programs in technology, management, commerce, design, and emerging digital fields with industry collaborations.
Does Yenepoya University provide industry exposure and practical training? +
Yes, the university emphasizes hands-on learning through clinical training, laboratory work, internships, industry collaborations, and real-world projects across both campuses.
Are there global certifications or industry partnerships available? +
Yes, several programs—especially at the Bangalore campus—offer global certifications and collaborations with organizations like IBM, Microsoft, IATA, and international professional bodies in finance and analytics.
What are the career opportunities after studying at Yenepoya University? +
Graduates can pursue careers in healthcare, IT, business management, research, and specialized industries, supported by strong placement assistance and industry connections.
